Who We Are

EarthProtein is a Kenyan biotech startup pioneering a revolutionary approach to sustainable and protein-rich livestock feed. We are dedicated to empowering Kenyan farmers and enriching the lives of livestock through innovative BSF breeding and premium BSF-based feed products.

Our Mission

At EarthProtein, we are passionate about building a thriving ecosystem for Kenyan agriculture. We achieve this by:
Empowering Farmers:
We provide Kenyan farmers with a reliable source of income through our high-demand BSF products.

Boosting Livestock Health:
Our BSF feed offers a natural, protein-rich alternative to traditional options, promoting healthier and more productive livestock.

Promoting Sustainability:
BSF farming reduces reliance on imported feed ingredients and minimizes environmental impact.

Our Vision

To be the leading producers and suppliers of BSF protein solutions in Africa.

What We Do

EarthProtein leverages cutting edge technology to breed BSF, transforming them into protein-rich livestock feed. We operate across the entire value chain, from breeding the BSF larvae to processing them into different types of feed.

How We Do It

BSF Breeding:
Our team of experts oversees a meticulous breeding process, ensuring the highest nutritional value in every EarthProtein product.

BSF Feed:
We offer a variety of BSF feed options, including fresh BSF larvae and dried BSF meal, to suit the specific needs of your livestock.

Technical Support & Integration:
Our dedicated team provides farmers with the knowledge and resources needed to seamlessly integrate EarthProtein feed into their existing feeding practices.

Meet Our Team

Lynn Odira - CEO & Co-founder

As the Cofounder and CEO of EarthProtein, Lynn is a highly skilled analytical chemist who brings a wealth of experience in laboratory testing and quality control to EarthProtein. Her dedication to scientific rigor ensures the consistent nutritional value and quality of EarthProtein's earthworm products.

Jackline Wangui - CTO & Co-founder

Jackline Wangui serves as the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) at EarthProtein. As a skilled statistician, Jackline leverages her expertise in data analysis and modeling to optimize EarthProtein's operations across the entire value chain. Her extensive experience working across various industries adds valuable perspective to EarthProtein's innovative approach to sustainable agriculture.

Hawa Waore - COO & Co-founder

Hawa Waore brings a unique blend of academic expertise and real-world experience to EarthProtein as the Chief Operating Officer (COO). As an agricultural economist, Hawa possesses a deep understanding of the Kenyan agricultural landscape and the challenges faced by farmers.
