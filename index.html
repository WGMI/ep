Earth Protein

Transforming worm to wealth

Who We Are

EarthProtein is a biotech startup pioneering a revolutionary approach to sustainable and protein-rich livestock feed. We are dedicated to empowering African farmers through innovative Black Soldier Fly (BSF) breeding to produce nutritious protein-based animal feed.

What We Do

EarthProtein leverages cutting edge technology to breed Black Soldier Fly, transforming them into protein-rich livestock feed. We operate across the entire value chain, from breeding the BSF larvae to processing them into different types of feed.

How We Do It

BSF Breeding

Our team of experts oversees a meticulous breeding process, ensuring the highest nutritional value in every EarthProtein product.

BSF Feed

We offer a variety of BSF feed options, including fresh BSF larvae and dried BSF meal, to suit the specific needs of your livestock.

Technical Support & Integration

Our dedicated team provides farmers with the knowledge and resources needed to seamlessly integrate EarthProtein feed into their existing feeding practices.
