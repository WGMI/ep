Our Team

Lynn Odira

CEO - Cofounder

As the Cofounder and CEO of EarthProtein, Lynn is a highly skilled analytical chemist who brings a wealth of experience in laboratory testing and quality control to EarthProtein. Her dedication to scientific rigor ensures the consistent nutritional value and quality of EarthProtein's earthworm products.

Jackline Wangui

CTO - Cofounder

Jackline Wangui serves as the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) at EarthProtein. As a skilled statistician, Jackline leverages her expertise in data analysis and modeling to optimize EarthProtein's operations across the entire value chain. Her extensive experience working across various industries adds valuable perspective to EarthProtein's innovative approach to sustainable agriculture.

Hawa Waore

COO - Cofounder

Hawa Waore brings a unique blend of academic expertise and real-world experience to EarthProtein as the Chief Operating Officer (COO). As an agricultural economist, Hawa possesses a deep understanding of the Kenyan agricultural landscape and the challenges faced by farmers. Hawa's passion for agriculture extends beyond theory. For over 3 years, she has been a successful poultry farmer, gaining firsthand experience in the realities of agricultural production. Furthermore, her work supporting farmers across different value chains has equipped her with a comprehensive perspective on the agricultural ecosystem.
